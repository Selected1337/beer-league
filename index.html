Champions Crowned: McChicken, Clifford & Rockwat Win Hopper Cup

THE POND — The Beer League preseason is officially in the books, and it ends with a bang: McChicken, Clifford, and Rockwat have secured the Hopper Cup after an undefeated run through the Minecraft Hockey Beer League’s preseason tournament.

The trio played with unmatched chemistry, dominating both ends of the ice with their blend of flashy offense and relentless puck pressure. But the real story? Clifford’s monster performance: 30 points in just 4 games — a stat line that already has league GMs whispering “MVP.”

"He’s a one-man faceoff circle, I swear," said Rockwat postgame. "We just let him do his thing and reap the loot."

McChicken served as the high-IQ playmaker, feeding perfect passes and baiting defenders with deceptive movement, while Rockwat brought physicality and goal-line grit that frustrated opposing netminders all tournament long.

As the regular season approaches, teams are already strategizing on how to stop this trio. If the preseason is any preview, they’ve got their work cut out for them.

For now, the Hopper Cup sits proudly in the winners’ locker — and the message is clear: these three mean business.
